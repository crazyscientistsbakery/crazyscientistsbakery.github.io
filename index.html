Who are we?

Welcome to the *crazy scientist's bakery*, home of the crazy food lovers!
We decided to set up this website in order to show more people what food really is and let them know more about it. We want the people who visit our website not only to learn a lot from it, but also find it pretty interesting. This website will contain articles, uploaded every week, about all kind of topics relative to food. If you have any good articles or your own food journals that you would like to upload on our website, just let us know!
